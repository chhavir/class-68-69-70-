About Us

seedling public school

The aim of the Seedling group is to create an ambience encouraging learning in a joyous environment and help students attain their educational and personal goals by developing their confidence and self-esteem. The environment nurtures our students and develops their personalities through innovative educational practices. The schools are equipped with state-of-art facilities, which enhance the intellectual, social and emotional development of students. We are ranked among the Top-5 schools of Jaipur, and provide international exposure to our students through international exchange programmes.
